This is a simulation that contains Plants(Green), Herbiovores(Purple), and Carnivores(Orange)
Plants reproduce after a certain amount of time, plants pass on their genes and their child may grow slightly slower or faster than them
Animals asexually reproduce after eating enough food, and can only reproduce every so often, they pass on their genes and their child may need to consume more or less food and reproduce slower/faster
If Animals run out of food, they die, animals will prioritize moving toward food if they can see it, otherwise the animals move randomly
